What is the UAE Golden Visa?

The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residency visa that allows foreigners to live, work, and invest in the UAE for up to 10 years. This visa is available to investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talent, and other key professionals, fostering a sense of stability and security for those looking to build a future in the UAE.

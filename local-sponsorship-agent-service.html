The Role of a Local Sponsorship Agent in the UAE

For many business activities in the UAE mainland, especially those outside free zones, a local sponsor is essential. The sponsor, often holding 51% ownership in specific business types, provides the legal framework required by UAE law. However, ownership control and profit distribution can be managed as per contractual agreements, allowing foreign investors to retain strategic direction.

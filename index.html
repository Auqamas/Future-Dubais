Steps to Launching Your Business in Dubai Mainland

1. Define Your Business Activity

The Department of Economic Development (DED) offers thousands of business activity options; choose the one that aligns with your goals.

2. Find the Ideal Location

Selecting a prime location is key to reaching your target audience and maximizing your market potential.

3. Choose Your Legal Structure

Dubai Mainland supports various legal structures, with the Limited Liability Company (LLC) being one of the most popular choices.

4. Appoint a Local Partner or Service Agent

Depending on your business type, you may need a local sponsor or agent to meet UAE regulatory requirements.

5. Secure Your Business Name

Choose a name that represents your brand and complies with Dubai’s naming guidelines.

6. Obtain Your Trade License

Submit your application to the DED to secure your Dubai Mainland trade license.

7. Set Up Your Office Space

Dubai Mainland businesses are required to have a physical office; serviced offices are a quick, fully equipped solution.
